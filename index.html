Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tool

How does the free trial work?

Our free trial gives you full access to all features of your selected plan for 14 days. No credit card is required to start. At the end of the trial, you can choose to upgrade to a paid plan or your account will automatically switch to our limited free version.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the changes take effect immediately. If you downgrade, the changes will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest. We are compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant privacy regulations. We never sell or share your data with third parties.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for non-profit organizations, educational institutions, and students. Please contact our sales team with relevant documentation to learn more about our discount programs.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer different levels of support based on your plan. Basic users receive email support with a 48-hour response time. Professional users get priority email support with a 24-hour response time. Enterprise users receive 24/7 dedicated support via email, phone, and live chat.

Can I integrate your tool with other software?

Yes, we offer robust API access and built-in integrations with popular software like Google Workspace, Microsoft Office, Slack, and many more. Professional and Enterprise plans include full API access, while Basic plans have limited API capabilities.
